# ✍  Biography
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;**Jiancheng Chi** received the B.Eng. degree in 2017 from Dalian University of Technology, Dalian, China, and the M.Eng. degree in 2020 from Tianjin University, Tianjin, China. He is pursuing a PhD degree in Computer Science and Technology at Tianjin University, Tianjin, China. He is a visiting scholar at Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology, Japan in 2023. His research interests include the industrial internet of things, industrial big data, edge computing, and machine learning. He has published some papers on top journals, such as IEEE Communications Surveys and Tutorials, IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing, Journal of Network and Computer Applications, and IEEE Network. He is a reviewer for several top journals, such as IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing, IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing, IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics, IEEE Transactions on Network Science and Engineering, and IEEE Wireless Communications Magazine. He has won many scholarships at Tianjin University including Chinese Government Scholarship, First-class Academic Scholarship, Lenovo Scholarship, and Outstanding Youth Scholarship. He is a student member of IEEE and ACM.
